Our Philosophy

At Judith's Kitchen we cook with love, integrity, and simplicity. Our goal is to bring out the most honest flavor from each ingredient.

Ask Judith

What's in the Curried Lentil Soup?

We start with red lentils and an onions sauteed in peanut oil, then we add a variety of South Asian flavors, including coconut and coconut milk

Everybody loves this soup! It's been our best seller since we opened and people come back for more all the time!

How do you make the flank steak so tender?

We marinate it in a combination of lime juice, salt, and herbs and then we grill it to just medium rare. This keeps it from becoming dry and preserves the juices.

Flank steak can be tough sometimes, so we make sure to marinate ours at minimum for 12 hours.

Everybody loves this dish, both for a main course and also in sandwiches and salads.
